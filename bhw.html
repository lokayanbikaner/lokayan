Lokayan's Heritage Walk’ got included in the Bikaner Camel Festival since 2017. The first Heritage walk of 2018 was on the second day of the Annual Camel Festival on 14th January, 2018. Witnessing the participation of more than 200 people for 3 hours of walk, covered around two kilometers in the desert city of Bikaner. The walk started off with Rajasthani traditional dance show on the road marking the beginning from Rampuria Haveli, covering Kothari Haveli, Sabzi Bazar, Amar Singh Rathod ki Rammat, Lord Laxminath temple, Bhandashah Jain Temple to Bikaji Ki Tekari.



We, at Lokayan have tried to carefully design, a heritage walk tour, with an aim to bring together for the travelers; the diverse culture, traditions & craftsmanship of Bikaner,in one wholesome journey. It actually tends to take you to journey that will let you encounter all the possible colors of Bikaner. This magnificent city is bestowed upon with both tangible as well as intangible heritage of its own kind. It is dotted with numerous havelis and other such architecture; exhibiting Rajputi, Mughal as well as colonial styles.
